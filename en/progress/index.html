Original Game Website

2/14/2018 - 2019

The first website I uploaded to be publically availible on the internet, on this very domain! See it in all of its 2018 glory here! The story behind this website is quite simple: my friends and I wanted to play flash games at school, however, the school firewall blocked most of them. So, I made a website that mirrored those same flash games to circumvent the firewall! Also, for whatever reason, I signed this website up to earn money from google ads on the page. Believe it or not, they didn't accept the page into their adsense program... no clue why

The 'upgraded' version of my previous website. With many, many more games, and a cleaner layout, this was my magnum-opus. It even had some JavaScript! The biggest weakness, however, was how every single page/game was added by hand, one-by-one. Also, as falsh was about to be discontinued, there was no real reason for me to keep this one going. Accessible here.
